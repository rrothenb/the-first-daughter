# Intergenerational Trauma Transmission

## The Pattern

- Pass down their unprocessed grief, anxiety, and attachment wounds
- Children become repositories for their unmet emotional needs
- Create family patterns of emotional volatility and religious anxiety

## Specific Mechanisms

- **Unresolved trauma** interfering with Eve's expectations and perceptions of her child
- **Inability to sensitively respond** to infant emotional cues
- **Compromised development of secure attachment** in [[Awan]]
