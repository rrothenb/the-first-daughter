# The Original Good - Reframing the Fall

### Original Human Moral State

- Natural empathy from mirror neurons
- Complete inability to dehumanize (everyone is family)
- Embodied moral intuitions from lived experience
- No abstract categories of "us" vs "them"
- Naturally compassionate and cooperative

### The Real Fall

- Not from innocence to knowledge
- But from natural goodness to corrupted judgment
- Humans taught to:
    - Judge rather than understand
    - Categorize rather than empathize
    - Follow abstract laws rather than embodied wisdom
- Development of "othering" as cognitive defense mechanism

### Why Awan's Line Was Destroyed

- Not because they were evil
- But because they retained original consciousness
- Threatened new order of abstract moral categories
- Maintained inability to "other" that conflicted with divine hierarchies
